How to Play

  • Players take turns to drop their discs into the grid.
  • Player 1 uses the red disc, and Player 2 uses the yellow disc.
  • The goal is to connect three of your discs in a row:
    • Horizontally
    • Vertically
    • Diagonally
  • The first player to connect three wins!
  • If all cells are filled and no one wins, the game ends in a draw.

Click "Restart Game" to reset the board and start a new game.
